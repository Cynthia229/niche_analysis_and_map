    From The Harley School

    Our school is a caring, close-knit community where students find their place, ignite their passions, find the joy in learning, and are prepared for success in our ever-evolving world.

    We help students learn to dig deep into the process of growing while uncovering what makes them thrive as learners. They are motivated to become people who explore with confidence, value imagination, and tackle challenges.

    Our academic program is designed to leverage and encourage the development of traits in a young persons mind, including: critical thinking, an enduring drive toward genuine understanding, and the ability to risk mistakes to fuel growth. Through a curriculum that emphasizes hands-on and project-based learning, our approach encourages students to value multiple perspectives and rise to complex challenges.

    More About The Harley School Academics

    Sustainability education at The Harley School

    We offer classes, projects, clubs, and electives to help students gain knowledge about sustainability from ecological, cultural, innovation, and economic perspectives. From our microfarm to our 15,000 sq. ft. living building, students have a myriad of opportunities for hands-on, experiential learning as they explore problems, seek solutions and build competencies for creating a sustainable world.
