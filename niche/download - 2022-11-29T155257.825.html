    I am impressed by the low student-faculty ratio at Greenhill. Teachers are always available for help before and after school. Greenhill offers many course offerings and clubs to meet lots of different tastes. While the sports teams may not place among the best, students are mostly able to play a sport if they want to. I love the diversity at Greenhill - the student body contains students from all over the world.
