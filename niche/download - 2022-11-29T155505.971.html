    From Berkeley Carroll School

    Rated by Niche as a top NYC private school with an A+ overall grade, Berkeley Carroll is known for providing a challenging education enriched with creative arts and athletics. With a 6:1 student-faculty ratio, one of the school's greatest strengths is that every student is seen, known, and appreciated. The strong, positive bonds between teachers and students are the cornerstone of a Berkeley Carroll education.

    100% of students go on to attend a 4-year college. In 2022, 92% of seniors were accepted to a college in the "Highly Selective" category and 82% were accepted to at least one of their two top choice colleges.

    Berkeley Carroll is an intellectual, creative and equitable community where devoted teachers challenge and engage talented students. The demanding curriculum and vibrant civic life prepare its graduates for success in college and for the greater endeavor—a life of critical, ethical, and global thinking.

    I have really enjoyed my time at Berkeley Carroll. I am a lifer at the school, and my time is all three divisions was/has been memorable and enjoyable. Lower School was fun and enjoyable---but it also sparked a lifelong love of learning and writing. BC's middle school offered a welcome contrast to the pop-culture understanding that the years between 5th and 8th grade are a tortuous nightmare; BC administrators and teachers nipped drama and conflict in the bud. The Upper School has been extremely rigorous, but I have also deepened friendships and met classmates from a diversity of backgrounds. As my time at the school draws to a close, the friends I made, and the passion of my teachers will stick with me for a lifetime.

    BC's distinctive Science research program, English & history electives, and American Studies course have prepared me for college and life post-grad. I learned how to write personal essays, review scientific literature, conduct rigorous research, and think critically
