    From Kent Denver School

    There has never been a better time to be a Sun Devil! Since our founding in 1922, Kent Denver has delivered the highest quality college preparatory experience in the Mountain West. More than 7,000 students have earned a Kent Denver diploma, thrived at top colleges and universities and gone on to lead full, meaningful lives.

    We believe students should have dynamic, spacious, state-of-the-art spaces that stimulate their curiosity, fuel their imagination and empower their best work. Our 200-acre campus includes: 

    * 100,000 sf of new learning spaces since 2018

    * 65+ classrooms, including 10 science labs

    * Innovation studios with 3-D printers, digital laser cutters, VR equipment, and tools to design/build almost anything!

    * Three performing arts theaters

    * Guitar lab and professional recording studio

    * Ceramics, metals, photography, drawing/painting and digital art studios

    * Two amphitheaters, two reservoirs and a boat house

    * Three gyms and 9 outdoor playing fields

    * Seven tennis courts

    I've attended Kent Denver school throughout high school, and am displeased to say that the experience was subpar. Though the school boasts its academic programs, it's abundantly clear that the only redeeming factors of their programs lie in the talented staff. Grades are often inflated, assignments promote busy work rather than creativity or problem-solving, and there exists an unhealthy culture obsessing over academic performance.

    Perhaps the most atrocious aspect of Kent Denver is its culture. Drugs and drinking are large problems on campus. Though the school promotes anti-drug use policies, students are not reprimanded for drug usage on campus or known MIPS/DUIS, especially if they're paying full tuition. Additionally, racism and discrimination run rampant on campus, as diversity in the school is near nonexistent.

    For parents, I urge you not to send your student to Kent Denver. The school is pretentious and problematic. Save your money for college!
