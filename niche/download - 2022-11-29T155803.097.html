    From Alabama School of Mathematics and Science

    The Alabama School of Mathematics and Science is our state's only fully public & residential high school for 10th-12th graders seeking advanced studies in math, science, computer science, and the humanities. ASMS serves students from all 67 counties. Tuition, room, & board are free as the school is funded by the state. ASMS is one of the only Cognia STEM-certified schools in Alabama and is considered a STEM legacy school in the U.S.

    ASMS is set apart by an accelerated curriculum, college-level academics, living-learning environment, leadership opportunities, and research focus not available anywhere else in the state. ASMS has a history of improving students' educational outcomes from enrollment to graduation -- standardized test scores, grades, college admissions offers, scholarship packages, etc. Our 30+ years of graduates are a testimony to this success as they lead the way in every sector - engineering, tech, cybersecurity, research, medicine, government, law, education, and more.

    ASMS Research Fellows Program

    ASMS offers academic opportunities not available to high school students anywhere else in Alabama, including a focus on research. Through our Research Fellows Program, students work with a faculty mentor to complete a research project and present it at the school's annual Spring Research Forum. Conducting research sets our students apart and helps them stand out when applying to college.

    I’m a current senior at the Alabama School of Math and Science, and I love it here! I love the higher-level STEM classes ASMS offers, such as Quantum and Relativity, Multivariable Calculus, Organic Chemistry, etc. Not many other schools offer these advanced classes, and it’s a great way to delve deeper into a subject you are interested in.

    ASMS has many other amazing opportunities like their Research Fellows Program. You get to work with a teacher on any topic you want to make a contribution to. It could be “Computational Studies of Mammoth Nuclear Genomes” or the “Evolutionary Prediction of South America’s Plant Diversity.” ASMS also has promotes leadership in our community through their Jo Bonner Leadership Academy. Juniors and Seniors learn how to develop their leadership skills and positively impact the community. They may also earn leadership positions, such as Ambassadors, SGA Officers, Peer Advisors, and more!

    Ultimately, you have the freedom to make your own path at ASMS.
