    From Oxbridge Academy

    Since opening in 2011, Oxbridge Academy has been known for its unique approach to learning. Serving grades 7-12, Oxbridge wants what students and parents want an education that inspires students to meet the world boldly. We teach whats worth learning, now and for the future. Building on a foundation in the core academic disciplines, the Oxbridge curriculum goes beyond, integrating the arts, athletics, health & wellness, community service, and hands-on learning in a multidimensional educational experience.

There are classes ranging from very basic to very advanced in all subjects, so the rigor of an individual student's courses is up to them.
