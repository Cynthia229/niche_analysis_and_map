    From MICDS - Mary Institute & St. Louis Country Day School

    For the 2022-23 school year, MICDS is full and no longer accepting applications.

    MICDS is a private, independent, Pre-K through 12th grade day school offering non-sectarian education. While living into our bold Mission, we strive to cultivate students who are equipped with academic, social and emotional intelligence. We shape future leaders who can contribute to a more positive, successful, and global society by growing the skills and strengths unique to them as individuals.

    Explore the outstanding opportunities available to our students ranging from arts, athletics, and STEM programs to humanities, global learning, and club offerings. Visit our website to learn about what it's like to be an MICDS Ram and part of the Rams Family!

    Please contact our Admission Office at (314) 995-7367 or admission@micds.org if you have any questions.

    Rating 5 out of 5  
    After joining the MICDS community freshman year, the first activity I partook in was the tennis team. Despite being the opposite of an athlete, I found myself loving every moment of practice, from team building activities to doubles matches. Academically, MICDS has classes in every field, from vector calculus, electricity and magnetism to the Modern Middle East.
    Despite being STEM oriented, I found my home in multiple classrooms, including in my calculus, physics, orchestra, and Modern Middle East classes. Pursuing contrasting interests and challenging yourself is at the core of MICDS. What is unique about our community, however, is the incredible amount of support and care the teachers give to students. Ive had teachers who Zoomed with me on weekends when I couldnt meet during their regular office hours and went out of their ways to check in and help me during busy times. The teachers dont only facilitate students academic development, they also get to know them as individuals
