    Charles E. Smith Jewish Day School is a top rated, private, Jewish school located in ROCKVILLE, MD. It has 913 students in grades PK, K-12 with a student-teacher ratio of 6 to 1. Tuition is $36,280 for the highest grade offered. After graduation, 97% of students from this school go on to attend a 4-year college.

But it can also be very demanding in terms of work and assignments and very stressful. Student life has some problems, there’s a lot of close-mindedness and not much diversity. You don’t have to be very religious to go there, but there is a rigorous dual-curriculum and mandatory judaic classes.

    Rating 5 out of 5  
    I can’t say enough great things about this school! Living in Virginia, I did not think attending a school in Maryland was even possible. The moment we stepped foot in CESJDS, I knew It was the perfect place for my son. It was risky switching schools his Junior year, but it was the best decision we made for him academically, spiritually, and socially. The teachers are very approachable and invested in the well-being of all students. There’s such a tight community and the students share a special bond. My son loves the small class sizes and the close relationships he has made. He loves starting his day with morning Minyan and learning in a pluralistic setting with his Jewish peers. I can go on and on about how this school has impacted my son in the best ways possible. To quote my son, “ I’m in the best place of my life!” If you are Jewish and want to give your children a meaningful, impactful, Jewish education, this school is for you!
