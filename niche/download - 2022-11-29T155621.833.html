    From Lake Highland Preparatory School

    Located on a beautiful 42-acre campus, Lake Highland Preparatory School is nestled in a scenic enclave of downtown Orlando. Here, Pre-K through 12th grade students receive a remarkable education and unique opportunities to learn and lead. Ingenuity is inspired in the classroom, and our nationally ranked academics, arts, and athletics prepare students to excel in college and beyond.

    We invite you to tour our campus, see our extraordinary facilities, experience our atmosphere of love and respect, and envision your child's success starting at Lake Highland.

    Lake Highland Arts

    Lake Highland provides a comprehensive visual and performing arts curriculum for prekindergarten through 12th-grade students. By developing a sense of creative exploration at a young age, Highlanders discover new interests through nearly 40 art classes and programs offered at Lake Highland. Expert faculty guide students to develop and display their talents in performing, visual, and digital art.

    Lake Highland makes sure its students have a perfect balance of studies and enriching activities outside of academia, such as sports, the arts, clubs, etc. Lake Highland lives up to its preparatory name, and it is well known that students tend to find university more than manageable, thanks to the school's rigor. Additionally, sports at the school have only become more and more successful, with even teams that used to find little success, such as football, reaching state championships. Luckily, students have beautiful facilities to thank for their academic and athletic achievements. Lake Highland recognizes the importance of facilities and has plans to create even more advanced ones in the near future. The only big takeaway from Lake Highland is its diversity. Though in recent years, the administration has attempted to cure issues stemming from lack of diversity, it is clear that the school does generally still hold a snobby, uninclusive culture.
