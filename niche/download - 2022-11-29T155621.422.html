    From St. Luke's School

    At St. Luke's, outstanding academics and strong community connections are inextricable. Our alumni cite relationships with teachers as the top value of their St. Luke's education, while parents speak of the caring attention and skills that distinguish our faculty.

    St. Luke's mission is to provide an exceptional education that inspires a deep love of learning, a strong moral compass, the commitment to serve, and the confidence to lead. St. Luke's Center for Leadership advances our mission by helping students develop valuable leadership abilities. A kind, inclusive community awaits you at St. Luke's.

    Advanced Curriculum

    When a particular Advanced Placement (AP) offering is strong, we offer it. But when our exceptional faculty know they can create more engaging, challenging, and compelling experiences, "Advanced Curriculum" courses are offered (21 are currently available to choose from). This gives brilliant teachers room to be brilliant and inspires ambitious students to swing for the fences academically.

    St. Luke's has been transformational for our family. One child graduated a few years ago and another is there now - they both started as students in St. Luke's 5th grade. My older child would cry before school in 4th grade which led us to start to look at new schools. Within 3 weeks of being at St. Luke's, his teacher identified a learning challenge that his prior public school had never noticed. We worked with St. Luke's to come up with a learning plan that was perfect. No more tears! In fact, this boy started to LOVE school. The creativity, care, and attention that the St. Luke's faculty demonstrates to students in small classes is so special. That same boy is now a student at a highly competitive college, playing a varsity sport that he discovered he had a talent for at St. Luke's, and he is thriving in school and in life. Our second St. Luke's student is having an equally powerful experience. St. Luke's is a wonderful school and we recommend it whole heartedly!
