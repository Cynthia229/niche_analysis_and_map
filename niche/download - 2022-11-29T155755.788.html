    From Rockingham County Schools

    Rockingham County Schools is a public school system in the Piedmont Triad Region of North Carolina that educates over 11,000 students across 22 schools. RCS features 12 elementary schools, including one year-round elementary school, four middle schools, and five high schools, as well as the Booker T. Washington Learning Center.

    RCS offers a diverse and comprehensive education to its elementary, middle, and high school students, including our SPLASH dual-language immersion programs at the elementary school level, our High School STEM and specialized career academies, and Rockingham Early College High School, which is partnered with Rockingham Community College.

    RCS empowers each child to be a life-long learner. Through effective relationships with family and community partners, Rockingham County Schools provides a safe, dynamic, nurturing, and innovative learning environment in which each child has the opportunity to reach his or her highest potential.
