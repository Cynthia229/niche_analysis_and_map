    From Second Baptist School

    Second Baptist School is a Christian, college preparatory school pursuing educational excellence for the honor of Jesus Christ. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to high achievement from pre-kindergarten 3 through grade twelve in academics, arts and athletics. At SBS, your child will be known individually by faculty that love God and care deeply about each other and their students. We believe a child’s gifts must be nurtured in a compassionate environment, allowing him or her to take risks and to be challenged to reach higher than ever thought possible. Upon graduation, SBS expects your child to possess the character, knowledge and skills to succeed in a digitally-based global society. Second Baptist School equips students to think critically, live biblically and lead courageously to impact the world for Jesus Christ.
