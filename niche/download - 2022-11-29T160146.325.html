    From North Cedar Academy

    North Cedar Academy (NCA) is a private, university-pathway boarding school located in Ladysmith, Wisconsin. NCA offers motivated and committed American and International students in grades 9 through 12 the opportunity to graduate from high school with a high school diploma and Associate of Arts & Science (AAS) degree simultaneously.

    The NCA model allows a student to obtain a Bachelors degree within two years of high school graduation, and a Masters degree within four.

    All NCA graduates who complete the Blugold Guaranteed Transfer Program (BGTP) are granted admission into the University of Wisconsin Eau Claire, as well as the University of Minnesota - Duluth, to finish their bachelors degrees!

    While many private boarding schools in the US offer AP Credits or an IB curriculum, NCA's BGTP students earn actual university credits and guaranteed credit transfer on a beautiful mid-western university campus.
