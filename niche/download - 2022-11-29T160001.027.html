    From International School of Indiana

    The Midwest's most distinct academics are the particular ISI combination of the world's "gold-standard" curriculum, the International Baccalaureate, which all of our students complete, and the state of Indiana's only full language immersion program that features three language-track options - French, Mandarin, and Spanish - for students starting at 3 years old as well as English as a Second Language (EAL) support for international students in all grades.

    With few rivals to our curricular approach nationwide, ISI is uniquely positioned to help students age 3 to grade 12 be Ready for the World Stage. No previous language studies are required for enrollment.

    More About International School of Indiana Academics

    Universal IB Curriculum

    The International Baccalaureate curriculum is considered the world's leading academic program for college readiness, the "gold standard" course of study that most schools reserve for only their top 5% of students or fewer. ISI proudly takes a different approach to the IB by extending its impact to all enrolled students, 3 year old through Grade 12, a model that we refer to as universal IB.
