    From Randolph-Macon Academy

    Located in Front Royal, Virginia, R-MA is an independent co-ed school for grades 6-12 with day and boarding options. We provide a superior university-prep curriculum with an elite Air Force JROTC program preparing students for lives of meaning and success.

    Each year, 100% of our students are accepted to top, globally ranked universities around the world. In the past 4 years, students have gained acceptances to Harvard, MIT, Cornell, UPenn, McGill, University of Toronto, UC-Berkeley, Johns Hopkins, Northwestern, Duke - and many more.

    The Class of 2022 graduated 59 students who received over $16.6 million in scholarships, of which 20 students received Service Academy appointments. The Class of 2021 also graduated 50 students who received over $12.3 million in scholarships.

    Randolph-Macon Academy is an amazing school. There’s no doubt about that. Its excellent Air Force JROTC program taught me what it means to be a true leader and to learn and respect everything our military does for this country.
