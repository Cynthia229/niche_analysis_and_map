    editorial

    Stoneleigh-Burnham School is a top rated, private, boarding, all-girls school located in GREENFIELD, MA. It has 150 students in grades 7-12 with a student-teacher ratio of 4 to 1. Tuition is $53,550 for the highest grade offered. After graduation, 100% of students from this school go on to attend a 4-year college.
