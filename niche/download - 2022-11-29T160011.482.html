    Crean Lutheran High School is a top rated, private, Christian school located in IRVINE, CA. It has 959 students in grades 9-12 with a student-teacher ratio of 9 to 1. Tuition is $18,330 for the highest grade offered. After graduation, 86% of students from this school go on to attend a 4-year college.

    There were so many unique courses that the school offers that I had considerable difficulty in fitting everything that I wanted to take. There are countless honors courses and AP classes that students can partake that can academically challenge and thus foster growth. There are also cohort programs ranging from those relating to engineering, business, law, and medicine. Being involved in these cohort groups may further allow students to explore their academic interest and potential career paths for the future.

    Crean is also a Lutheran based Christian school that strives for excellence in academics through Jesus Christ. If you want your child to be a well rounded individual who has a positive influence on his community through his/her well developed understanding of love in Christ, enroll him/her at Crean!

    As a student at Crean Lutheran, I have enjoyed my time here. The academics are top notch and there are lots of courses to choose from. The staff and teachers are also super friendly and are great people! I also love the clubs offered on campus and how they promote people starting up their own clubs. Our student body or ASB is outstanding as im currently a part of it and have loved planning events and getting our student body involved with events, games, and other activities that go on on campus. As a member of the Swim and Water Polo teams, I have loved being a part of these teams and bonding with my teammates and coaches. The only major issue I have is how our Aquatics programs never gets proper funding. Even after promising a pool years prior, we still do not have one and have to use a pool that is 20+ minutes away from the campus.
