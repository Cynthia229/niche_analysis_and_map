    Rating 4 out of 5  
    In my time at Oak Park, I have found that the school is very keen on what the expectations will be in college, so they uphold those expectations so that we are not shocked when we get there at the pressure or intensity of classes. There is a wide range of electives, class levels, and teachers available to educate and grow students. At times, the pressure of the school can become a little intense, but there is almost always a teacher that understands the intensity and aids students in managing the pressures of high school. One of the greatest assets available to students is their peers. Through team sports and activities, the friends you make are guaranteed to be the best part of your four years. Overall, Oak Park is an academically rigorous school that is manageable if you find the right people to surround yourself with.
