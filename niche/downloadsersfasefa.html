    Instilling Goodness Elementary & Developing Virtue Secondary Schools is a top rated, private, boarding school located in UKIAH, CA. It has 205 students in grades K-12 with a student-teacher ratio of 4 to 1. Tuition is $8,520 for the highest grade offered. After graduation, 85% of students from this school go on to attend a 4-year college.

    Rating 3 out of 5  
    My rating would be a 5-star had it not been for the flaws that came along my school experience. The academics are good, lending us sufficient knowledge we needed to know to move onto other grades. The classes are fairly small compared to other schools, allowing teachers to pay more attention to every one of us. The students in my class are humorous, kind, and sometimes too energetic. Class rivalry doesn't exist with this type of atmosphere.
    During the Pandemic I began to notice the downside. A few of the teachers were overly strict/only paid attention to their expectations rather than whether we were being engaged or not. While most of the teachers were kind and aware, others entirely slacked off from their job. The Education Board didn't involve parents when they made decisions and immediately turned down the suggestions of families. Even when the school was slowly falling apart, they kept pushing for unreasonable demands.
    In the end, I give 3.5 stars. It could have been better.
