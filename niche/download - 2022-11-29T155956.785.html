    From Minnetonka Senior High School

    Minnetonka High School focuses on world-class, child-centered excellence. Ninety-nine percent of seniors graduate, and 86% are college-bound. Set in the beautiful Lake Minnetonka region, the high school serves nearly 3,400 students and is the one of the most sought-after for open enrollment in Minnesota. Minnetonka High School offers Chinese and Spanish immersion, advanced computer coding, dozens of AP and IB courses, a distinguished collaborative research program, an innovative advanced professional studies program, flexible online learning options and award-winning arts and music programs. It has also expanded its offerings in technical education to include a range of courses in the trades.

    Rating 3 out of 5  
    Academically, Minnetonka excels in everything they do. The teachers are well educated and always willing to help students in need. Beyond the teachers, the Minnetonka school district does not focus on the student population, but on the parents/guardians of the students. Their main focus is to make families happy while students remain miserable. Their are many great opportunities at Minnetonka, but this school focuses on students who were privileged growing up and will continue on that same path throughout their lives. With this, the school tends to neglect students who have lacked support from their families throughout their k-12 experience. Limited resources are available to students who fall behind. It is easy to get forgotten about at this school. Minnetonka seems like an inclusive community where every student is happy. Sure, it looks shiny on the outside, but when you actually experience it yourself, you see how lonely it really feels.
