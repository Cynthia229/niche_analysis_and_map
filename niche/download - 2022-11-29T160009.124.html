    From IMG Academy

    With a seamless methodology that cultivates academic growth, athletic improvement, character development & social responsibility, IMG Academy prepares student-athletes to become tomorrow's leaders & influencers, both in sport & in life. IMG's prestigious history combines elite athletic accomplishments with unparalleled college placement, creating an overall campus environment that’s both supportive yet challenging. IMG's championship culture transcends more than just athletic fields and courts. Student-athletes from around the world enroll with a common goal of pushing themselves to reach their personal best.

    IMG’s globally diverse atmosphere allows each student-athlete to grow & develop unlike anywhere else, & the on-campus energy is both invigorating & contagious. Over 1,200 student-athletes are enrolled at IMG between 6-12 graders and post-graduates. Every student-athlete who enrolls at IMG has an undeniable passion for excellence & an unrelenting drive to surpass all expectations.

    Rating 5 out of 5  
    The athletic facilities are top notch and we’ve had a great experience scholastically. If your kids are focused/motivated, they can take themselves as far as their athletic ability and effort allow. There are some amazing D1/pro caliber athletes. However, there are opportunities for kids who are above average athletically. If you love your sport and are willing to work, playing for a university is a real possibility. An advantage to IMG is having a medical facility for diagnosing injuries and an athletic training staff/facility to address injuries through PT. Our experience with coaches has been positive. Our kids improved at their sport and coaches have played them appropriately. Teachers care, and the academic/athletic counselors have been helpful. Administrators are adept at providing ideas for making curriculum decisions. If your kid is motivated to play a sport, IMG is a great option. Having everything athletic, scholastic and medical in one place is convenient and hard to beat.
