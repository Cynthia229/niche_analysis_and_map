    Rating 5 out of 5  
    THS is a unique gem. If you have seen the movie "Dead Poet's Society," imagine a school where every teacher is some version of the Robin Williams character in that movie: passionate about teaching, invested in the students' lives, teach the curriculum plus so much more about living an authentic life with integrity. They take complete responsibility for their students' performance - the word "tutor" does not exist at this school. If a student needs extra help, the teacher delivers it.

    The leadership is unparalleled. The principal, Mr. Perkinson, leads with great intellect, compassion, common sense, and humor. He embodies the global perspective of the school, and is most committed.

    The college counselor, Ms. Burler, was a high priced private counselors to wealthy families. With the small size of the school, this is a benefit you get without having to pay those exorbitant fees. She provides personalized guidance to get every student into the best school for that student.
