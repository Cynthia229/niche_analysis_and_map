    From Veritas Collegiate Academy

    Veritas Collegiate Academy: One School, Many Campuses (Chesapeake and Arlington, VA; Beijing and Kunshan (Shanghai) China; and Budapest, Hungary). Veritas Collegiate Academy (formerly Veritas Christian Academy) is a multi-campus global Classical Christian school, founded in 2004, with a vision of graduating young men and women leaders. We train students to think clearly, logically, and listen carefully with critical discernment. Our students gain understanding and the ability to reason persuasively and articulate themselves precisely. Ultimately, graduates will be capable of evaluating life experiences in the light of Scripture, and they will do so with eagerness, and joyful submission to God. Our Average 2021 SAT Score is 1360, one of the highest in our region, and our graduates regularly get admitted into the top 40 universities in America.

    I have attended Veritas for 4 years, and am absolutely loving my senior year. I am very thankful to Mr. Elgut for all his help with my courses and the college process. Probably my favorite thing is the toughness of the courses, preparing me for college.
