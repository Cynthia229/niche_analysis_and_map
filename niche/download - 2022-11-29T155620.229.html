    Finding the right educational home for your child is so important. We hope that Tower Hill is a part of your search. For questions or additional information, please feel free to contact our office at 302-657-8350.     Upper School Experiential Learning - Tower Term

    Every year during the last week of school, Upper Schoolers embark upon an immersive learning experience we call Tower Term. Tower Term is a limitless space where the Tower Hill community can explore learning together in an interdisciplinary, experiential way. Recent courses include: Backpacking in Shenandoah; Knit Chat; Life Among The Bees; and Biking, Camping and High Ropes Adventure!
