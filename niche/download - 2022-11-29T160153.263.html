    From La Salle College Preparatory

    Our college preparatory program enables diverse and academically capable students such as yourself, to become extraordinary people. La Salle College Preparatory is a Roman Catholic educational community inspired by the traditions of Saint John Baptist de La Salle.

    We are insistent on educational excellence that focuses on:

    NURTURING THE TALENTS OF EACH STUDENT

    The uniqueness of each students spiritual, intellectual, physical, and artistic potential is recognized and promoted.

    INSPIRING IN STUDENTS A SENSE OF RESPONSIBILITY FOR OTHERS

    Motivated by a spirit of faith and zeal, our students are informed by and made responsible for the world in which they live.

    CHALLENGING STUDENTS TO EMBRACE DIVERSITY

    Students are challenged to demonstrate respect and appreciation for people of different talents, cultures, religions, genders and economic backgrounds.

    MOTIVATING OUR STUDENTS TO RESPOND COMPASSIONATELY

    Students demonstrate compassion towards the needs of others, especially the poor.

    I really enjoyed La Salle's wide breadth of activities, especially their clubs, and felt like there was always a place for someone to find their niche. The teachers can be hit or miss sometimes and can really make or break a class, especially at the AP level. I had some really amazing teachers that helped shape who I am today, but also some really bad ones. The overall spirit of La Salle is extremely inclusive and their service aspect is quite special and unique compared to that of other schools. Socially, the school does tend to attract people clumping together in groups and cliques like any other high school.

    Rating 5 out of 5  
    La Salle College Preparatory is a school unlike other pasadena high schools. La Salle has students take advanced courses sooner so they are better prepared for College and have more time to broaden the minds. The school's student body is co-ed, very diverse with a total student capacity of around 450 students. Because of the small size of the school students are able to learn in a smaller class size and have more one-on-one learning with teachers who are always eager to help. The school has progressed far in it's STEAM courses, there is even a brand new room dedicated to nothing except STEAM course like: Robotics, Drafting, Innovation and Design, and Architecture. With all of these great achievements and advancements, there is not anything that needs to change at La Salle.
