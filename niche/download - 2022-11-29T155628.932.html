    More About Flint Hill School Academics

    Flint Hill Offers Stellar Robotics Program

    Our robotics curriculum allows students to place engineering design, tech literacy and mathematics in a tangible context. Beginners design, build and program robots; later, they work together to create robots with specific functions. In advanced courses, students design 3D printed pieces. Our award-winning robotics team competes at the college level by building complex unmanned vehicles.

    Rating 5 out of 5  
    The one thing Flint Hill has that most High Schools do not is an extensive Robotics lab. The lab has private servers for coding classes, materials for building robots, and space for testing. My Freshman year in High School, I was fortunate enough to take Robotics as one of my elective classes, and my first impression became a rush of desire to find out, and problem solve. This class allowed me to live out my dream of working with a team, and allowed me to create something that is all my own. My Robotics teacher impressed me most of all with his considerable knowledge, and through watching him I developed a passion to be just as knowledgeable. I experienced challenges in the form of deadlines that had to be met within a limited time, but it was here that I learned how much time needs to be put into something for it to be successful. My task was to design the Robots which would take movable objects and stack them on top of one another; and after each challenge.
