    From Germantown Academy

    GA offers a one-of-a-kind experience for students from early childhood to 12th Grade. Our welcoming nonsectarian, coed environment invites students to ask questions, push beyond comfort zones, and prepare for the next phase of their lives.

    Investing in an independent school education is one of the most important decisions any parent can make for a child. The value of a GA education is immeasurable. We invite you to learn about the unique people, programs, spaces, and traditions that make GA the right choice for your child to realize how Anything is Possible Starting Here.

Hands-on experiences and project-based learning enable students to develop, see, feel, assess, and refine their ideas in tangible ways. Accordingly, GA offers innovative environments that transform students into active, engaged learners. Whenever children discover their passion, we have the capacity to let them pursue it.

    We have found a vibrant and supportive community at Germantown Academy. As a parent, I have been impressed with the faculty's ability to meet my children where they are by providing encouragement and support when necessary and challenge and rigor when appropriate. Focus is on critical thinking and analytical skills, working with others in groups and peer-review situations. Students are taught to advocate for themselves and contribute to the community. My children feel comfortable sharing their ideas and asking questions. In Upper School, teachers make time to meet with students on an individual basis, and this is encouraged. The school works hard to make families feel welcome, There are many ways to get involved, such as volunteering, social events, attending sports, community service opportunities, and educational speakers for parents.

    Both our daughter and son went to Germantown Academy. Now they are attending top tier liberal arts colleges. Germantown Academy develops well rounded students while offering top rated education. The honor and AP classes (some are taugher by former college professors) are very challenging and rigorous. The director of the school’s college counseling was very helpful at my children’s college application.
