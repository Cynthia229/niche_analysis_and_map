    editorial

    Springfield Commonwealth Academy is a top rated, private, boarding school located in SPRINGFIELD, MA. It has 120 students in grades 6-12 with a student-teacher ratio of 6 to 1. Tuition is $57,900 for the highest grade offered. After graduation, 95% of students from this school go on to attend a 4-year college.
