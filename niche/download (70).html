    From BASIS Independent McLean

    When students love to learn, they excel! At BASIS Independent McLean (BIM), we seek to empower your child with a well-rounded education - one that encourages curiosity, exploration, and discovery as we prepare each child for the next step in their educational journey.

    BIM, home of the Red-Tailed Hawks, is an age 2–grade 12 private school that raises the standards of learning to the highest international levels in a liberal arts and STEM-focused curriculum benchmarked to the top education systems worldwide. Passionate educators with subject-specific expertise inspire students to love learning and develop the knowledge, confidence, and autonomy to be successful in school and beyond.

    We believe that students can do more than what is typically expected of them and create a joyful learning culture where hard work is celebrated and intellectual pursuits result in extraordinary outcomes. At BIM, we will challenge each child to realize their full potential as they prepare to soar!

    My kids have been attending Basis since before the pandemic, and we are so impressed! Rich academic curriculum, good inclusion of the arts, excellent opportunity for differentiation based on student needs. The teachers are dedicated and so are the parents to the kids' success. While the administration has been bumpy as the school has gotten established (only 5 years old), they earned their stripes during the pandemic. Organized, safety-focused and diligent about communication. We supplement school with sports leagues and scouting outside of school, which many parents do. Easy to do given the extensive choice you have in the DC area. Also appreciate the international study body with impressively bright students from all over the world. A real meritocracy, unlike other private schools that admit based on VIP status or connections/donations. In the next few years, this school will only continue to advance its place in the rankings.

    My middle schooler has been going to Basis for 3 years now. We love it! It should be stressed however that it is not for everyone. It is very rigorous and academically inclined. If your kid’s preference is sports or extra curricular activities rather than academia then this school isn’t for you. This school focuses immensely on academics. The teachers are extremely committed to the kids. My child has found a level of focus that I personally didn’t know she had before attending Basis. I couldn't be happier, but it does require commitment from the parents and the student(s). I see a couple of negative comments and I really believe its mostly due to parents expecting for the material to be easier. I’ve been to PTA meetings were some parents want less rigor in academics and more sports. I find that hilariously insane cause this is not that type of school; its not for everyone, but then again, neither are ivy league schools.
