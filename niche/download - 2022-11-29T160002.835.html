    Our daughter just completed her first year at Darlington. Her 3rd Grade experience was amazing! She loved the small class sizes and individual attention she received. She made many memories with special seasonal activities, educational outings, and cultural experiences. It's clearly apparent how deeply the faculty and staff care for the students. Darlington also offers The Learning Center, which is an invaluable resource when students need a little extra help.

    Even with a less than ideal Spring semester, due to the pandemic, Darlington continued to provide a superb education. The assignments were meaningful and the daily live digital interactions between students and teachers helped everyone stay connected. There were even opportunities to continue service, wellness, literacy, and leadership projects from home during distance learning. We highly recommend Darlington School to anyone seeking a unique experience and the highest standards for their child's education.
