    editorial

    Allen Academy is a top rated, private, boarding school located in BRYAN, TX. It has 340 students in grades PK, K-12 with a student-teacher ratio of 6 to 1. Tuition is $12,642 for the highest grade offered. After graduation, 100% of students from this school go on to attend a 4-year college.
