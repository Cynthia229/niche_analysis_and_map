    From Silicon Valley International School

    Silicon Valley International School provides a one-of-a-kind bilingual International Baccalaureate experience. We are an independent, co-educational, non-sectarian day school with Chinese, French, and German programs spanning preschool through 12th grade. We offer opportunities for students who are fluent in a second language and those who would like to learn a new language.

    Since 1979, Silicon Valley International School (formerly known as International School of the Peninsula and Alto International School) has been the top international school in Silicon Valley, which educates students through a rigorous and dynamic bilingual program. Students are inspired to develop an inquisitive and responsible international mindset, one that fosters curiosity and builds cultural fluency, encouraging positive action and change in our world.

    INTL is an International Baccalaureate World School offering the IB Primary Years, Middle Years, and Diploma Programme.

    With two middle schoolers who started in the Nursery program (pre pre-k), we have experienced the entire program at International. Our children have received a top-notch education and are natively fluent in French to the point that they had no problem attending French public school when we lived abroad.

    The language immersion is excellent and the school has adapted teaching methods over the years, particularly in math and then moving into the IB program. The school has a vibrant community and highly-engaged parents who support each other and the school, from classroom help to fundraising efforts.

    There have been times when we were mildly frustrated with obtuse communication from the administration, but we never questioned the quality of care of the teacher. Their focus is on the whole student and creating thoughtful world citizens. They also focus on creativity and arts, as evidenced by the impressive annual Arts Night created by the kids. You can't go wrong with SV International!
