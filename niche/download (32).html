    From Georgetown Day School

    Georgetown Day School is a Washington, DC preK-12 private school with a city campus and a comprehensive, innovative curriculum. Our "GDS Student Will" competencies outline the essential skills our students will need to have a meaningful impact in the world; these skills lie at the heart of the GDS curriculum.

    A GDS Student Will:

    * Build networks & collaborate across difference

    * Innovate & create

    * Take risks & learn from failure

    * Self-advocate

    * Think critically

    * Communicate clearly & powerfully

    * Tackle complex problems

    * Learn actively & resourcefully

    * Engage as a just, moral, ethical citizen

    * Lead

    GDS Policy & Advocacy Institute

    The Policy & Advocacy Institute is a student-led, interactive learning experience where fellows use their passion for a subject matter to shape the world around them. Over the summer, High School students learn about pressing social justice issues by meeting with advocates, engaging in conversation with policymakers, and building relationships with those impacted by the issue they are examining.
