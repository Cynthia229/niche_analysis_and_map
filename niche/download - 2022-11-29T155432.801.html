    Rating 5 out of 5  
    Cistercian is best known for providing rigorous academics for bright boys, but it is much more than that. At Cistercian they focus on the formation of the boys. This formation includes challenging academics, supporting physical development in athletics, and exposing them to arts and culture that inspire their creativity. They do all this while cultivating their spirituality and fostering their moral and social growth. This formation is best supported by the unique Form Master relationship (advisor that travels with the same group of boys 5th-12th grade). Cistercian will challenge your son to reach beyond what he thought was possible while doing so in a safe and supportive environment. The brotherhood the boys form within their class is deep and long lasting. Cistercian “gets” boys, and everything they do has a purpose in the journey to manhood. Cistercian is a blessing to our family, and we love watching our two sons pursue their passions and thrive at this wonderful school.
